id: troubleshooting-camera-black-screen
title: Black Screen
summary: Troubleshooting steps for black screen or live view failure.
order: 20
keywords: [camera, black screen, preview failed, live view, plugin, connection limit]
---

1. If live view shows a black screen or fails, check whether it is caused by a browser/plugin issue. Uninstall and reinstall the plugin.
2. Check whether the maximum connection limit has been exceeded.
3. For 1/2/3 series devices, the maximum number of connections is 6. For 4/5/6/7/8 series devices and PTZ cameras, the maximum number of connections is 20. Live view may fail after the limit is exceeded.
