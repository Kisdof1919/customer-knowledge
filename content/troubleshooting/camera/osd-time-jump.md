title: OSD Time Jump
summary: Troubleshooting steps for camera OSD time jumping forward or backward.
order: 60
keywords: [camera, osd, time, ntp, timezone, dst]
---

1. Confirm whether the NTP parameters are configured correctly and provide a screenshot of the NTP settings.
2. Confirm whether the device is connected to multiple time synchronization sources, such as multiple NVRs, DVRs, or platforms.
3. Confirm whether the configured time zone matches the local time zone and provide the time zone configuration.
4. Confirm whether daylight saving time is enabled and provide a screenshot of the DST settings.
5. Confirm whether there was any manual time synchronization caused by user operation.
