id: troubleshooting-camera-offline
title: Camera Offline
summary: Troubleshooting steps for camera offline or intermittent disconnection issues.
order: 10
keywords: [camera, offline, disconnect, poe, network cable, switch, ups]
---

1. First check whether the network cable is defective. Connect the faulty camera and another normally powered camera to the same switch using the same network cable for comparison. If the cable is confirmed to be defective, replace it with a known good cable.
2. If the cable is normal, check whether the switch load is too high or whether the PoE power budget is insufficient.
3. Check whether the UPS power supply is unstable.
4. Check whether the network port is damaged or short-circuited.
5. If the issue may be caused by a software program exception, collect the required information and send it to HQ for analysis.
